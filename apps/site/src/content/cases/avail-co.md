title: Avail.co
company: Avail.co
role: Lead product designer
period: "2021–2023"
order: 5
problem: "Avail.co's rental storefront pages had fragmented, inconsistent structure that was depressing conversion and requiring engineering involvement for every content update. Onboarding and payment flows had friction points driving landlord churn across a platform serving 700K+ customers."
approach: "Redesigned all storefront pages from scratch — simplified structure, unified components into a reusable library. Built a lead-generation feature connecting renters interested in buying their first home. Improved onboarding and leasing/payment workflows to reduce friction for both landlords and tenants."
outcome: "70% increase in user conversion. Marketing team now updates pages 84% faster without engineering dependencies. Lead-gen solution generating 10K+ qualified leads per month across 5M active users."
retrospective: "Avail was acquired by Move Corporation — Realtor.com's parent company — and became a sub-product of the Realtor.com ecosystem. Realtor.com rental listings started surfacing within Avail's landlord experience, and Avail's property management tools were recommended to landlords coming from Realtor.com. The rental domain expertise I built here became the foundation for my work on Realtor.com/Rentals."
