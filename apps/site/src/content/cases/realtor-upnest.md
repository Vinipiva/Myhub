title: "Realtor.com — UpNest"

role: Senior Product Designer
period: "Jan 2025 – May 2025"
order: 3
problem: "UpNest had structural and hierarchy issues that slowed agents' daily workflows across the full funnel. Agent onboarding was fragmented, lead segmentation was unclear, and contextual actions were buried — making it hard for agents to manage their pipeline and understand where they stood in the sales funnel."
approach: "Promoted from the Online Store work to lead UpNest's UX revision. Redesigned agent onboarding and lead management flows. Overhauled CRM structure, hierarchy, and interaction logic across platforms. Surfaced contextual actions at the right moments and restructured lead segmentation for clarity."
outcome: "Agents completing tasks 40–60% faster across the funnel. Improved agent satisfaction and NPS. Better lead tracking and pipeline visibility. More coherent onboarding path for agents entering the Realtor.com ecosystem."
retrospective: "UpNest was a product with a lot of accumulated debt — structural decisions that made sense individually but didn't add up to a coherent experience. What I'd do differently: push harder for a unified information architecture review before touching individual flows. The onboarding work surfaced upstream issues that slowed us down mid-project."
