No dedicated team, no roadmap — and a product running on an abandoned Salesforce platform. Working with the design manager and senior manager, I led research that uncovered quick-win pain points and shaped a 6-month roadmap. Quick wins were deliberately chosen to also align the platform with Harmony's design language, laying the groundwork for a full future migration. The rebuild — on Salesforce LWC with Knotch, Amplitude, and session replay instrumentation — focused on three fronts: smarter navigation, richer product education, and mobile-first responsiveness. Mobile drop-off cut from ~90% to under 10%. The work justified team expansion and led to my promotion to UpNest.
